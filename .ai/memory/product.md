# Product

An AI-powered content repurposing web app that turns user-provided source
materials into a finished, platform-tailored social-media post. Built for
Blazity's "AI for Content" hackathon.

## Purpose

Remove the manual busywork of adapting one piece of content for different
audiences. The user supplies raw materials and picks a target platform; the app
returns a complete, ready-to-publish post matched to that platform's audience.

## Core flow

1. User inputs raw materials (text/notes).
2. User selects the target platform (e.g. LinkedIn, TikTok, YouTube).
3. The app generates the tailored post.
4. The user copies the finished output to the clipboard — no manual editing.

## Key differentiator

Audience-aware tone and format adaptation per platform is the central value, not
generic summarization: professional and value-driven for LinkedIn; entertaining
and hook-heavy for TikTok/YouTube. The output must be good enough to publish as-is.

## Target users

People who repurpose source material into posts across multiple channels —
content creators, social-media managers, and marketers.

## Hackathon framing

Judged on thinking over volume: one thing done well, explainable, and demoable.
The AI must be aimed at the right problem with its output checked.
